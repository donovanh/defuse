But a question that often comes up, is how do we think about animation on large projects. How do we make it consistent. Can we use animation to help the UX of what we build?

Of course. Animation can help in many ways. Whether it's showing or hiding content, or drawing attention to what matters to guide our visitors.

Animation also speaks to us on a deeper level. We can take a lamp, and it's just a lamp, but make it move... and people love the lamp.

Stripe example

It can also help brands. A recent example was Stripe, who famously took a boring payment modal and gave it character through lots of subtle animations.
